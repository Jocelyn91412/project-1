Agent Kim Reactivated

Agent Kim Reactivated follows a mild-mannered single father who must resurrect his hidden life as a lethal special agent when his teenage daughter suddenly disappears. Forced out of his quiet routine, he relies on his elite military training to navigate a dangerous criminal underworld. His relentless search pits him against corrupt elites and secret agencies, transforming his desperate rescue mission into a high-stakes battle for survival.
